Harnessing Data and Technology for Environmental Conservation and Healthcare Advancements

Published: 2023-07-31

In today’s news, we bring you updates on various topics related to open data, information, and knowledge. Our aim is to keep you informed about the latest developments that can help you leave a better world for your children.

First, let’s talk about the Climate Smart Shrimp (CSS) program developed by Conservation International (CI). This program focuses on supporting sustainable shrimp aquaculture while restoring mangrove ecosystems. To identify suitable sites for the program, machine learning and earth observation data were used. Factors such as sea level rise, flood risk, infrastructure access, and historical mangrove cover were considered. The project has developed a web-map tool that analyzes the potential suitability of aquaculture sites based on specific criteria. This tool will help engage farmers, industry, and communities and attract investment to scale the CSS program. It’s exciting to see how data and technology can contribute to environmental conservation efforts.
