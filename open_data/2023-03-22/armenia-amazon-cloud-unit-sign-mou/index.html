Armenia , Amazon cloud unit sign MoU

Armenia's Ministry of High-Tech Industry and Amazon Web Services (AWS) have signed a Memorandum of Understanding with the aim of accelerating the adoption of cloud services in the public and the private sector of the country. The MoU aims to accelerate the modernisation and digital transformation of its state.
