Education Secretary leads call to tackle antisemitic abuse on campuses

A summit is being held to discuss how to protect Jewish students and staff from antisemitic abuse on university campuses across the UK during the Holocaust Memorial Day. They are among those who have been victims of antisemitism in higher education.
