Europe Leading the Way in Sustainable Technology and Data Innovation

Published: 2024-04-11

Europe is a leader in sustainable technology. The open-source community focuses on funding, AI tools, sustainability, governance, and data.

Events like FOSDEM and initiatives like OpenLLM Europe show innovation. Prioritizing data openness, compliance, and carbon impact is key for progress.

The Department of Levelling Up, Housing and Communities supports open data standards like Open Referral UK and SAVVI. They invest in initiatives to set standards, starting with housing through the iStand project.

Global collaboration is important for food security. Initiatives like HiHi, DVI, and OCOPP help countries diversify crops and improve agriculture.

DTCC partners with REGnosys for efficient data management in the financial sector. The goal is to streamline regulatory processes using the Common Domain Model and ISO 20022 format.

Organizations like CIMMYT focus on sustainable agrifood systems. They prioritize open data, knowledge sharing, and local engagement to promote sustainable agriculture.

Working together for a better future through open data and sustainability is crucial. Transparency, accessibility, and collaboration can lead to a more sustainable future.
