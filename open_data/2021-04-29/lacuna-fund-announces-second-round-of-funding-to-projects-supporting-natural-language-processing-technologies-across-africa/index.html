Lacuna Fund announces second round of funding to projects supporting Natural Language Processing technologies Across Africa

The Lacuna Fund has announced a second round of funding to projects supporting natural language processing technologies across Africa. The project aims to fuel artificial intelligence in languages such as speech tagging, sentiment analysis, emotion analysis and facial recognition in low resources nations.
