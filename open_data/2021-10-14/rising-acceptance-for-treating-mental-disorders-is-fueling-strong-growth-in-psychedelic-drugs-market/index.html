Rising Acceptance For Treating Mental Disorders is Fueling Strong Growth in Psychedelic Drugs Market

US media reports from the US state of Maryland say the growth of the drug-assisted psychotherapy market is expected to reach a forecast rate of 14.5% in 2026, according to research and market figures. Scientists have warned that the development of new medications could lead to an unmet need for treatment.
