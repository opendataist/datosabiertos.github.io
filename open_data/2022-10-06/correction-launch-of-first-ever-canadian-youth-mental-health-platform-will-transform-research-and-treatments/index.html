CORRECTION -- Launch of first - ever Canadian youth mental health platform will transform research and treatments

The Canadian youth mental health project has been launched by RBC Future Launch. The project aims to increase the understanding of teenagers and their needs and improve assessment and treatment approaches. A pioneering project will enhance Canada’s overall Understanding of Mental Health Insight (CYMHI).
