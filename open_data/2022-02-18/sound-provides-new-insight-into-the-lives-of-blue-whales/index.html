Sound provides new insight into the lives of blue whales

Scientists from the Monterey Bay National Marine Sanctuary (MBAR) have learned how blue whales communicate with us, using sound recordings released from a remote marine observatory in the southern coast of England. The BBC's Tom de Castella has been speaking to scientists.
