NHS pressure cause temporary closure of Minor Injuries Unit as Covid cases rise

A minor injury unit at Evesham Hospital has been temporarily closed due to the spread of a rare coronavirus outbreak in the north of Herefordshire and Worcestershire valleys, the NHS has confirmed. However, it remains open for the bank holiday weekend.
