Ireland ranks top 5 in Europe for digital performance

Ireland has become the first country in Europe to be ranked in the European Development Index ( DESI), according to the latest European Commission assessment of digital infrastructure and digital services (DESI) published in Strasbourg on Wednesday. Irish government officials have welcomed the release.
