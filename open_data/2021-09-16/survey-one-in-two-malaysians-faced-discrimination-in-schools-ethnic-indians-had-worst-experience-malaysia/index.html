Survey : One in two Malaysians faced discrimination in schools , ethnic Indians had worst experience | Malaysia

More ethnic Indians in Malaysia feel they suffered from discrimination in the country’s education system compared to others, a recent survey by the Sekolah Semua Youth movement has revealed, and says that teachers are responsible for their experiences.
