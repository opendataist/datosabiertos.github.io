Quebec reports 31 COVID - 19 deaths as hospitalizations dip below 2 , 000

The Canadian province of Quebec has reported a sharp drop in the number of people in hospital with coronavirus, according to official figures released by the Ministry of Health. The government says it will end its mask mandate for indoor public spaces on May 14.
