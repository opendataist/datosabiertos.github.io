Report on identification and Geographical Information System ( GIS ) mapping of open spaces for humanitarian purposes in Bhimeshwor municipality - Nepal

Nepal's government has launched a national survey to identify suitable open spaces in five municipalities of five earthquake affected districts in Bagmati and Gandaki Provinces. The project aims to enhance the decision-making process at the municipal level.
