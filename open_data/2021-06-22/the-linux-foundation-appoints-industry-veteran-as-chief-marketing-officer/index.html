The Linux Foundation Appoints Industry Veteran as Chief Marketing Officer

Derek Weeks has been appointed chief marketing officer (SVP) at the Linux Foundation, according to the latest announcement from the company's parent company Sonatype, in San Francisco, California, US media reports say. Before, he was a co-founder of All Day DevOPS.
