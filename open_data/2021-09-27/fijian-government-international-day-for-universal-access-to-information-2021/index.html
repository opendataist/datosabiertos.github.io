Fijian Government - INTERNATIONAL DAY FOR UNIVERSAL ACCESS TO INFORMATION 2021

Fiji has become the first country in the world to declare 28 September as a day for access to accurate and reliable information, according to the countrys chief executive, Manmohan Singh. When the coronavirus pandemic took place in February, the government said it would raise awareness of the need.
