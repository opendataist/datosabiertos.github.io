Natural History Museum : £2bn ROI from digitisation project

Scientists could be able to access the world’s most valuable natural history collections, according to a new report published by the National Heritage Foundation (NHM) in January. They believe that the digitalisation of the ancient treasure trove would create benefits of £2 billion over three decades.
