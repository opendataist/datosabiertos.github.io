Coronavirus Update : Canada suspending flights from India and Pakistan for a month

The Canadian government is suspending incoming flights from India and Pakistan for a month as cases of COVID-19 surge in both countries. The government says it is calling for the vaccine to be prioritised for expectant mothers and those who contract the virus are at higher risk.
