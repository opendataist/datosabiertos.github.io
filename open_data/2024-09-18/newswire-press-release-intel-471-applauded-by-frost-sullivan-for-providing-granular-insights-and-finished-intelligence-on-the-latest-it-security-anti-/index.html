Newswire & Press Release / Intel 471 Applauded by Frost & Sullivan for Providing Granular Insights and Finished Intelligence on the Latest ... - IT Security / Anti - Spam / Cybersecurity - Frost & Sullivan

Intel 471 s CTI platform has been recognized by Frost & Sullivan with the 2024 Global Enabling Technology Leadership Award for its ability to help organizations navigate complex cyber threats, improve visibility across digital assets, and proactively secure their environments. The company, a leading global CTI solution provider, extends the impact of threat intelligence across various business functions, addressing use cases like threat hunting, security operations, and governance, risk, and compliance (GRC). Intel 471 s cyber intelligence cloud platform provides granular insights and finished intelligence on the latest cyber threats through a unified web portal or API integration. Its external attack surface management (EASM) solution enables organizations to discover vulnerabilities and monitor trends regarding newly weaponized CVEs. The SaaS platform s core intelligence domains include adversary intelligence, malware intelligence, credentials intelligence, vulnerabilities intelligence, marketplace intelligence, and cyber geopolitical intelligence. Intel 471 s Collection Management Team (CMT) offers unique insights into attackers intent and motivation, aiding in the swift detection and prevention of breaches. This saves investigation time and costs and prevents payments for illegitimate ransom demands. By providing timely insights and detailed leak information, Intel 471 helps organizations understand breaches and adjust strategies promptly, enhancing their security posture and mitigating risks effectively. Intel 471 has established itself as a leading global provider of CTI solutions through its human-centered intelligence approach. The company enables organizations beyond just technology, serving as a trusted partner and offering services like RFIs and threat hunt packages, supported by a team of expert analysts who provide comprehensive insights and protection against sophisticated threats. For its strong overall performance in the CTI industry, Intel 471 earns Frost & Sullivan’s 2024 Global Enabling Technology Leadership Award. Intel 471 empowers enterprises, government agencies, and other organizations to win the cybersecurity war using real-time insights about adversaries, their relationships, threat patterns, and imminent attacks relevant to their businesses.
