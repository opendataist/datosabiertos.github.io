Procurement Notices - UNDP - HQ - 01234 - 1 . 4 . 3 Purchase of 3 laptops for the Amman is Listening Project

The United Nations Development Programme (UNDP) is collaborating with cities and local governments to demonstrate their active role in implementing Agenda 2030 and advancing the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). One notable example is the UNDP s support for the Great Amman Municipality s Amman is listening project, a winner of the 2021 Global Mayors Challenge. This open data participatory platform aims to better understand residents needs and provide feedback on local services and events. The platform allows Amman s residents to offer real-time feedback on critical city services, which can help optimize service provision and manage crises by translating resident-driven data into knowledge for local decision-making. Suppliers interested in participating must submit their offers directly in the system, following the instructions provided in the solicitation document and the available user guide. If you haven t registered before, you can create a profile using the registration link shared in the procurement notice and following the guides available on the UNDP website. To find the specific tender, use the search filters and subscribe to the tender to receive notifications about any amendments to the tender document. If you need assistance with the online system, contact the provided contact details in the solicitation document. This initiative is part of the UNDP s SDG Action Campaign and Procurement Team s efforts to support local governments in achieving the SDGs.
