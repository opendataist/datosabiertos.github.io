Moody to Acquire RMS , Leader in Climate & Natural Disaster Risk

Moody’s has entered into a definitive agreement to acquire RMS, the leading global provider of climate and natural disaster risk mapping and analytics, for approximately $2.0 billion. The acquisition will accelerate the development of its global integrated risk capabilities.
