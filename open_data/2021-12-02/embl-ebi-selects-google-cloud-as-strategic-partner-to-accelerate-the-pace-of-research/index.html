EMBL - EBI Selects Google Cloud as Strategic Partner to Accelerate the Pace of Research

Google has been chosen as the world's most comprehensive set of freely available and up-to-date molecular data resources, according to a new partnership between the scientists and researchers. Scientists are being urged to use Google Cloud to accelerate the pace of service delivery to the global user community.
