Cranbrook Launches New Data Portal for Easy Access to City Information

Published: 2023-11-23

The City of Cranbrook has a new Open Data Portal. It lets people easily find city-related information. This is a big step towards being open and accessible.

The Open Data Portal has different types of information that can be downloaded for free. This includes things like garbage and recycling schedules, maps, and details about the land. It also has updated photos and cards for property services.

One good thing about this portal is that it is always being updated. This means that people can always find the newest information. The City of Cranbrook wants to give its community the facts they need to make good choices.

The Open Data Portal is part of a global trend. For example, the Irish government made their data available online. They want to build trust and encourage new ideas.

Open data is helpful in many fields, like technology and science. When data is open, researchers and inventors can use it to help society.

In business, open data helps investors and decision-makers. Futu Holdings Ltd., the company behind Moomoo, a finance platform, had a successful quarter. They give their users tools and information to make smart investments.

Moomoo has 21.09 million users and 1.65 million paying clients. They show how useful data can be. They help investors understand the complicated world of finance.

Overall, the Open Data Portal in Cranbrook and the global trend of open data show how important it is to be open and accessible. When information is easy to find, people can make good choices. This leads to new ideas and a better world for the future.
