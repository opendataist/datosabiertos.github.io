Malta's Times of Malta and Wikimedia Community Join Forces to Bring Knowledge to the Masses

Published: 2023-10-20

Times of Malta has partnered with Malta’s Wikimedia community to bring open data to the forefront of news reporting. The collaboration aims to empower readers with knowledge by sharing information with the broader Wikimedia community and educating staff about open data. This innovative approach to journalism marks the first of its kind in Malta and sets a precedent for other news organizations to follow.

In Canada, data from various sources, including Environics Analytics, Open Data Toronto, and Statistics Canada, has been used to compile rankings for neighborhoods. This data provides valuable insights into different areas and can help inform decision-making processes. By making this data accessible to the public, it promotes transparency and allows for a more informed and engaged community.

Meanwhile, the United Arab Emirates (UAE) is making strides towards achieving its goal of net-zero emissions by 2050 through a transition to a circular economy. The country has established a Circular Economy Committee to promote investments in circularity and accelerate the adoption of circular economy practices. This commitment to sustainability is further supported by the emphasis on Open Data policies, which enable the sharing of information and insights to drive innovation and progress.

At GITEX GLOBAL 2023, experts discussed the role of digital technologies in transforming public services, with a particular focus on the benefits of Open Data. This event showcased the latest innovations, including a smart refrigerator that offers enhanced connectivity and convenience for UAE families. The integration of digital technologies and Open Data can address major challenges in various industries and pave the way for a more sustainable future.

These examples highlight the power of data, information, and knowledge in driving positive change. By embracing open data principles, organizations and communities can foster transparency, collaboration, and innovation. This not only empowers individuals with valuable insights but also enables them to make more informed decisions that can shape a better future for generations to come.
