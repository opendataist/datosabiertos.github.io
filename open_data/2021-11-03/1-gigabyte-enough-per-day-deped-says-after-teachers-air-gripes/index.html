1 gigabyte enough per day , DepEd says after teachers air gripes

The Philippines government has maintained a lack of access to internet data for teachers, according to reports from the Inquirer newspaper on Wednesday. They say the data capacity is not enough to access e-learning and department applications, but they say it can support eight hours of videoconferencing.
