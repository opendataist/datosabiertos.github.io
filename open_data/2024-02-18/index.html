Embracing Data: Advancements Shaping Our Future

Published: 2024-02-18

Recent developments in data and information have shed light on global affairs, technology advancements, and national strategies. Sri Lanka’s Air Force Commander plans to strengthen the country’s fleet with new aircraft like C130 and Beach King Air models. Despite its focus on militarization, the country continues to increase defense spending to maintain a strong military presence. The Air Force aims to protect the Indian Ocean airspace for peace and sustainable development. However, concerns linger about the military’s actions in the Tamil North-East region, where a tragic armed conflict caused many casualties.

Mapping apps are now essential tools in our daily lives, though they can sometimes have mistakes. The Overture Maps Foundation, founded in 2022, works to improve mapping accuracy by using data from different sources. The Global Entity Reference System boosts data compatibility, and generative AI brings new possibilities for map datasets. Collaborations with big companies like Google and Microsoft, as well as automakers like Hyundai, show a bright future for Overture Maps. This project, supported by the Linux Foundation, aims to make open data more accessible for better mapping services worldwide.

Experts stress the importance of open data, open compute, and open models in India’s AI journey. Initiatives like the India Datasets Platform and the National Data Governance Policy are crucial for fostering AI innovation. To reduce biases, local startups should create models that suit India’s unique landscape. Despite funding gaps in the AI startup scene, there are calls for more government investments in large-scale AI projects. The proposed partnership between public and private sectors to build supercomputing and quantum computing hubs aims to tackle the challenges hindering AI deployment in key sectors, boosting productivity and global competitiveness.

As the world deals with data use and tech advancements, the focus on open data, accurate information, and customized solutions is vital. These developments shape our present and have the potential to create a better future for future generations.
