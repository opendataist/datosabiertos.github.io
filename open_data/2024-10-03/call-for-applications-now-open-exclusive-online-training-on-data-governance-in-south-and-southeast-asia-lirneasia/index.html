Call for applications now open : exclusive online training on data governance in South and Southeast Asia LIRNEasia

LIRNEasia announces an online training program on Data Governance in South and Southeast Asia, scheduled for December 10-11, 2024. The program aims to educate early to mid-career professionals on data governance laws and policies in the region, with a focus on influencing policy and communicating research to policymakers. Participants will gain insights into data governance frameworks, best practices, and policy-making processes in countries like India, Indonesia, Nepal, Sri Lanka, Thailand, the Philippines, and Pakistan. The training is free but requires participants to commit to two half-day sessions and post-course evaluations. Selected participants will be part of a network of policy intellectuals working on data governance in the region. LIRNEasia, supported by the International Development Research Center, Canada, will provide resources and expertise for the program. Interested individuals should apply by October 16, 2024, through the provided link. Selection will be based on past work in digital policy-making and the potential impact of the training on their work. The program seeks to build a cohort of policy intellectuals in the region, with LIRNEasia committed to nurturing this group and creating opportunities for further engagement.
