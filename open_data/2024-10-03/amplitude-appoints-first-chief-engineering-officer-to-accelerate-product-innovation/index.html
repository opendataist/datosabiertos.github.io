Amplitude Appoints First Chief Engineering Officer to Accelerate Product Innovation

Amplitude, a prominent digital analytics platform, has announced the appointment of Wade Chambers as its inaugural Chief Engineering Officer (CEO). With over 25 years of engineering leadership experience, Chambers will spearhead Amplitude s Engineering division and bolster its product innovation capabilities. This development comes after the recent launch of Amplitude Made Easy, Snowflake Native Amplitude, and enhanced data governance features. Chambers, who has been an advisor to Amplitude since 2016, brings a wealth of knowledge in the digital analytics sector. He has previously held leadership roles at Included Health, Twitter, Proofpoint, Yahoo, Opsware, and Netscape. His expertise in driving innovation, fostering collaboration, and delivering impactful results at leading technology companies will be instrumental in expanding Amplitude s integrated platform, which currently includes Analytics, Session Replay, CDP, and Experiment. Amplitude CEO and co-founder, Spenser Skates, expressed his enthusiasm for Chambers joining the company in-house. Skates highlighted Chambers first-principles thinking, mentorship, and engineering leadership skills, which are crucial for the company s growth stage. Chambers appointment aligns with Amplitude s mission to help companies unlock the power of their products by providing self-service visibility into the entire customer journey. With more than 3,200 customers, including Atlassian, NBCUniversal, Under Armour, Shopify, and Jersey Mike s, relying on Amplitude, the company aims to guide them in capturing trustworthy data, uncovering clear insights about customer behavior, and taking faster action. For more information about Amplitude and its platform, please visit amplitude.com.
