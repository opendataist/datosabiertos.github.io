Q3 2025 EPS Estimates for Veeva Systems Inc . Raised by Zacks Research ( NYSE : VEEV )

Zacks Research analysts have raised their Q3 2025 earnings estimates for Veeva Systems Inc., now predicting $1.05 per share, up from $1.02. The full-year earnings consensus is $4.20 per share. Other analysts have also adjusted their price targets, with Bank of America increasing their target to $240.00, Robert W. Baird to $251.00, Piper Sandler to $230.00, and Guggenheim to $233.00. The stock has a market cap of $34.78 billion, a P/E ratio of 63.37, and a beta of 0.81. Institutional investors own 88.20% of the company s stock, and insiders hold 10.50%. Recently, SVP Jonathan Faddis sold 2,353 shares at an average price of $200.00. The company provides cloud-based software solutions for the life sciences industry, including CRM, content management, analytics, and patient data services.
