Smart Postpaid to raise the bar for mobile experiences with exclusive perks for subscribers

Smart Communications, Inc. (Smart) has enhanced its postpaid plans with exclusive lifestyle perks, cutting-edge gadgets, and entertainment access to elevate mobile experiences for subscribers. These benefits are available to new and existing Smart Postpaid Plan 999 and up users, offering instant redemption of perks across categories like Dining, Travel & Leisure, Beauty & Wellness, and Shopping through the Smart App. Subscribers can enjoy discounts and complimentary dining at popular establishments, unique travel deals, premium beauty services, and a 20% discount on sports and lifestyle footwear at Capital. Smart Postpaid users also receive a complimentary Netflix mobile subscription, with PHP 149.00 savings per month, and a P200 off on Cignal Play TV Box with a three-month premium Cignal subscription. Smart Postpaid continues to offer data-packed plans with the latest smartphones from global manufacturers, along with gadget add-ons and more payment options. The company aims to roll out more perks and partner brands, providing subscribers with an upgraded lifestyle experience. Starting at Smart Postpaid Plan 999, subscribers can enjoy these benefits, including Unlimited 5G data, Unlimited Calls & Texts, Unlimited Landline Calls, and 20GB of open-access data. Smart s network has been recognized for delivering the Philippines Best 5G Coverage Experience. For more details on exclusive perks and to sign up for a Smart Postpaid Plan, visit www.smart.com.ph/perks or head to the nearest Smart Store.
