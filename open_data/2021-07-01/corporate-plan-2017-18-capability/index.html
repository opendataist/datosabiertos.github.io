Corporate Plan 2017 - 18 : Capability

The National Library of England has announced a new strategy to improve the workforce and ensure the diversity of its staff is maintained by the government. The Strategic Workforce Plan is to focus on developing internal leadership capabilities, career sustainability and building internal capability.
