Globe provides free COVID - 19 medical insurance coverage through its digital services

The Philippines' broadband company Globe has announced it is offering free Covid-19 and dengue insurance coverage for new and existing subscribers starting March 26 and April 4 across the entire island of Luzon, which has been hit by the coronavirus pandemic.
