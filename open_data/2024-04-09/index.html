SiFive made a new system called HiFive Premier P550 for AI and tech. It will come out in July 2024. The system has a strong 64-bit processor with P550 CPU cores. It has 32KB L1 cache, 256KB L2 cache, 4MB L3 cache, and 16GB DDR5 DRAM. It also has a GPU, video encoding, and neural processing. It runs on Ubuntu Linux and has many I/O ports.

At the University of Arkansas, there will be Undergrad Research Week from April 15-19. Students can join info sessions, panels, and get advice. They will learn about research, libraries, Python, and STEM. The week ends with a symposium showing student work. They will also learn about ScholarWorks@UARK for their thesis.

Prof. Joshi spoke at a seminar at the University of Jammu on open data for librarians. Open data helps make info more available. Librarians play a key role in this. Prof. Gupta from the University of Jammu praised Prof. Joshi’s talk. The seminar showed how open data helps share info and knowledge.

These stories show how open data helps innovation and understanding. Being open and sharing info leads to collaboration and progress. It helps create a more connected and informed society.
