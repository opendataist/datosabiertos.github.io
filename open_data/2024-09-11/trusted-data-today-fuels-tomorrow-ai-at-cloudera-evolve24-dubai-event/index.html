Trusted Data Today Fuels Tomorrow AI At Cloudera EVOLVE24 Dubai Event

Cloudera, a leading hybrid platform for data, analytics, and AI, is hosting the EVOLVE24 conference in Dubai. This event aims to bring together industry leaders, customers, and partners to explore strategies for enhancing data-driven insights and productivity in the era of generative AI. The conference will feature breakout sessions, keynote speeches, and hands-on workshops, focusing on the value of modern data architecture, the benefits of hybrid cloud, and the acceleration of enterprise AI. Sponsored by IBM, Amazon Web Services (AWS), and Red Hat, EVOLVE24 Dubai will address challenges and barriers of enterprise AI adoption, hybrid data management, and the state of data infrastructures. The event will provide attendees with real-world insights and practical applications, with keynote sessions featuring senior executives from Cloudera and leading voices from various industries. Cloudera, with over 25+ exabytes of data under management, is the most robust hybrid open data lakehouse for analytics and AI. The company s platform enables organizations to efficiently and securely manage large volumes of data, trust its accuracy, and rely on it for analysis and AI-powered decision-making. EVOLVE24 Dubai is a unique opportunity for Cloudera to connect with its customers globally, offering hands-on training and one-to-one access with Cloudera experts. The event series aims to empower enterprises to transform data into valuable insights, while maintaining privacy and ensuring responsible AI deployments. For more information about EVOLVE24 Dubai, visit Cloudera s events page. About Cloudera: Cloudera is the only true hybrid platform for data, analytics, and AI. With 100x more data under management than other cloud-only vendors, Cloudera empowers global enterprises to transform data of all types into valuable, trusted insights.
