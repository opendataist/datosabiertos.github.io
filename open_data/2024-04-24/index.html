Investing in Quality Data for Development Progress

Published: 2024-04-24

Investing in good data is important for progress in developing countries. Dr. Mohammed Amin Adam says data helps with challenges and should be a priority. Ghana is improving its data systems and wants more help to use data for good.

E2open Parent Holdings, Inc. got Catena-X certification for its supply chain work in the car industry. This helps connect car makers and suppliers better and makes the supply chain more clear. E2open wants to make a platform that can change as needed to be more efficient and sustainable.

Snowflake Inc. made Snowflake Arctic, a large language model, to compete in AI. They want to improve AI and give customers more choices. Snowflake is focusing more on AI solutions.

The ILO’s Evaluation office helps make decisions and provides evidence to support decent work and social justice. Councillors met in Oxford to talk about cleaning rivers to stop pollution.

Science Moab’s Science on Screen event at Swanny Park teaches about natural science on the Colorado Plateau. Local scientists lead discussions and show movies. This event aims to build partnerships and teach science in a fun outdoor setting.

Using open data policies helps drive innovation and make society better. We need to use data for good changes and growth.
