SkyFi Launches Open Data Integration and Geospatial Analytics Platform, China Leads in NEV Industry, Concerns Raised Over Ethical Practices in Renewable Energy, Asian Stocks Await U.S. Inflation Data, IBM Showcases WatsonX AI Platform, Bitget Announces Smart Awards 2023 Winners, Thailand Promotes Open Data Sharing in Medical and Agricultural Sectors

Published: 2023-09-13

SkyFi, a leading Earth Observation (EO) company, has launched two new products that are set to revolutionize the geospatial data industry. Open Data, one of the products, integrates Sentinel 2 data into its platform, providing free and open geospatial data for collaboration and innovation. This move promotes transparency and accessibility in the field of Earth Observation, allowing researchers, scientists, and innovators to work together to uncover hidden patterns and gain valuable insights from the data.

Another product, SkyFi Insights, is a geospatial analytics platform that enables users to explore visuals, track changes over time, and gain a deeper understanding of the EO data. With global imagery updated daily, SkyFi aims to be the go-to hub for geospatial knowledge. This platform empowers users to make informed decisions and drive innovation in various industries, including agriculture, urban planning, and environmental conservation.

In another part of the world, China has emerged as the global leader in automobile production, consumption, and export. The development of new energy vehicles (NEVs) has played a crucial role in this achievement. NEVs, along with advancements in energy storage, big data, cloud computing, and intelligent connected vehicle technologies, are creating a promising future for a comprehensive “vehicle-energy-infrastructure-cloud” industrial ecosystem.

China has also established the world’s largest network for battery charging and swapping, ensuring reliable energy infrastructure for smart vehicles. However, there is still a need to improve infrastructure to address the inconvenience of battery charging and swapping. NEVs not only contribute to green transportation but also promote the energy storage sector, making them an essential component of China’s sustainable development goals.

To make NEVs smarter, intelligent road infrastructure and real-time cloud computing services are necessary. China is actively promoting technological innovation and cross-border collaboration to support the high-quality development of the NEV industry. By leveraging data and technology, China aims to create a more sustainable and efficient transportation system for the future.

In the realm of renewable energy, the annual RE Plus conference is currently taking place in Las Vegas, bringing together clean energy professionals from around the world. However, concerns have been raised about the involvement of companies with alleged ties to forced labor in China’s Xinjiang region. It is crucial to ensure that companies adhere to ethical practices and do not engage in human rights violations.

Moving on to the financial markets, most Asian stocks traded flat or lower as investors awaited key U.S. inflation data. However, Chinese property stocks saw strong gains as Country Garden won approval to extend debt payments. Apple Inc. experienced a decline in its stock price after unveiling its new range of iPhones, disappointing some investors. The focus now shifts to U.S. inflation data and the Federal Reserve meeting, which will have a significant impact on global markets.

In the field of artificial intelligence and data analytics, IBM showcased its latest AI and data platform, WatsonX, at the Think 2023 event in Mumbai. WatsonX aims to ease enterprise AI burdens and enable the deployment of trustworthy AI at scale. This platform empowers businesses to leverage AI throughout their operations, driving substantial business value and unlocking new levels of productivity.

In the world of cryptocurrency, Bitget, a top crypto derivatives and copy trading exchange, announced the winners of the Smart Awards 2023. These awards recognize outstanding achievements in the world of crypto investments and provide valuable insights to the investor community. By analyzing on-chain data, Bitget aims to honor exceptional performance and promote transparency in the crypto market.

Lastly, the National Electronics and Computer Technology Center (Nectec) in Thailand is focusing on promoting open data sharing in the medical and agricultural sectors. By developing a large language model (LLM), Nectec aims to enhance the value of businesses, drive innovation, and stimulate economic growth. Open data sharing has the potential to revolutionize various sectors, including healthcare and agriculture, by leveraging the power of data for the benefit of society.

In conclusion, the world of open data is rapidly evolving, with new technologies and platforms emerging to promote transparency, accessibility, and innovation. From geospatial data to renewable energy and financial markets, open data is driving progress and shaping the future. By harnessing the power of data, we can create a better world for future generations.
