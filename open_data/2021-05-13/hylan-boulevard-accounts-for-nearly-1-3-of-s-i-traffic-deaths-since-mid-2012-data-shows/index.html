Hylan Boulevard accounts for nearly 1 / 3 of S . I . traffic deaths since mid - 2012 , data shows

More than one-third of all traffic fatalities on Staten Island since mid-2012 have occurred in crashes where Hylan Boulevard was listed as the primary or cross street, according to city data. Another significant crash has been linked to a high speed limit on the roadway.
