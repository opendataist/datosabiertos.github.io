Tearing Up the Track : Australia - Based Sprint Analytics & PARIS Tech Team Up to Offer Collaborative FP & A / Performance Management Solutions

The main conclusion from the article is that PARIS Technologies International, Inc. has entered into a strategic partnership with Sprint Analytics, an Australian data consulting and analytics firm, to expand its reach and offer enhanced financial and other data modeling solutions in Australia. This partnership leverages Sprint Analytics experience and expertise in FP&A and performance management solutions, particularly their use of Olation® and PowerExcel software. The partnership aims to address the challenges faced by organizations using traditional FP&A products, such as difficulty in migration, time consumption, and high costs. Sprint Analytics team, with over 40 years of experience in delivering high-value data solutions, will utilize PARIS s platform to offer real-time data access and planning through PowerExcel. PARIS Technologies International, Inc. will benefit from Sprint Analytics depth of experience in creating BI and Planning solutions, while Sprint Analytics will gain access to PARIS s collaborative platform to deliver effective and performant solutions to their clients. The partnership aligns with both companies missions to provide great analytics and planning solutions to their clients. The collaboration is expected to create many successful customers across various industries, including hospitality, manufacturing, professional services, not-for-profit, and healthcare. For more information on PARIS Technologies International, Inc., visit www.paristech.com. For more information on Sprint Analytics, visit sprintanalytics.com.au.
