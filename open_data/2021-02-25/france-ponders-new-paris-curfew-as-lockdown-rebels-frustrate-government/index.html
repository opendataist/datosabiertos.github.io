France ponders new Paris curfew as lockdown rebels frustrate government

French Prime Minister Jean Castex has confirmed that a night curfew could be imposed on Paris and the surrounding region amid government frustration that too many people are ignoring lockdown rules as Covid-19 infections soar. The government has said it is unbearable to see other French people flouting restrictions.
