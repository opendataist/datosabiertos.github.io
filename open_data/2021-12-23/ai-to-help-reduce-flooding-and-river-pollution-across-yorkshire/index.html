AI to help reduce flooding and river pollution across Yorkshire

Scientists have developed artificial intelligence (AI) that can predict blockages in sewers during heavy rainfall, which can flood homes and gardens with wastewater. Yorkshire Water is rolling out the new AI to its network of more than 2,000 combined sewer overflows (CSOs) in a bid to reduce pollution risks.
