BlueVoyant Report Reveals Ransomware is the Number One Cyber Threat facing Higher Education

Ransomware attacks against universities and colleges have increased by 100% between 2019 and 2020, according to a new report published by BlueVoyant, the largest cybersecurity threat in the world, and the number one cyber-attack in higher education institutions.
