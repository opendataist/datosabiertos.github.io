AI helps get aid to uncharted regions of the developing world

The WorldPop team collaborated with the African Field Epidemiology Network to address the issue of unvaccinated children in a region of Katsina State, northern Nigeria. Due to the lack of good maps in the area, they utilized available data to organize a door-to-door campaign to ensure children received routine vaccinations. Google scientists acknowledged the use of lower quality satellite images in some parts of the world, which could lead to potential inaccuracies and limitations in the maps. The AI model used for mapping requires cloud-free images for optimal results, but this can be challenging in cloudy climates and may result in missing tiny structures like informal shacks. Google emphasized the importance of maps in providing essential services and aiding decision-makers in crisis response. By creating accurate maps, they aim to help decision-makers comprehend the current environment and ensure that everyone is reached. The text also encourages learning more about Global Health Security to protect oneself and one s family. (Word count: 149)
