Smart Postpaid to raise the bar for mobile experiences with exclusive perks for subscribers

Smart Communications, Inc. (Smart) has enhanced its postpaid plans with exclusive lifestyle perks, cutting-edge gadgets, and entertainment access to elevate mobile experiences for subscribers. These benefits are available to new and existing Smart Postpaid Plan 999 and above users, offering instant redemption of perks in categories like Dining, Travel & Leisure, Beauty & Wellness, and Shopping through the Smart App. Subscribers can enjoy discounts and complimentary dining experiences at popular establishments, unique travel and leisure deals, premium beauty services, and footwear and wardrobe upgrades with exclusive discounts. Smart Postpaid users also receive a complimentary Netflix mobile subscription, P200 off on Cignal Play TV Box, and access to premium Cignal content. The latest flagship devices from global smartphone manufacturers are offered with data-packed plans, and gadget add-ons are available to empower subscribers with the latest tech innovations. Smart Postpaid is powered by Smart s superior mobile network, recognized for delivering the Philippines Best 5G Coverage Experience. To avail these perks, subscribers can start with Smart Postpaid Plans+ 999, which includes Unlimited 5G data, Unlimited Calls & Texts, Unlimited Landline Calls, and 20GB of open-access data. For more details and to sign up for a Smart Postpaid Plan, visit Smart s website or head to the nearest Smart Store. Smart Communications continues to roll out more perks and partner brands for its subscribers, offering an upgraded lifestyle experience beyond just a mobile plan.
