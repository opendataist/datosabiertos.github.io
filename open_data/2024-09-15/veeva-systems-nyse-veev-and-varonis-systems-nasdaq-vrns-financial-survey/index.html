Veeva Systems ( NYSE : VEEV ) and Varonis Systems ( NASDAQ : VRNS ) Financial Survey

Veeva Systems and Varonis Systems are both technology companies, but Veeva Systems appears to be a better investment based on several factors. In summary, Veeva Systems outperforms Varonis Systems on 11 of the 14 factors compared, making it a more favorable investment.
