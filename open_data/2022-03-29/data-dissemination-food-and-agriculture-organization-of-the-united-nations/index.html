Data dissemination| Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations

The Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO) has launched an open data licencing policy to ensure that data is free and unrestricted to access to all corporate databases, including the latest published by the World Food Organisation (WFO) in October. The FAO is responsible for gathering and dissemining a comprehensive range of data.
