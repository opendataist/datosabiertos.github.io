Pioneering a secure and collaborative future in united kingdom conveyancing – Mortgage Strategy

Angela Hesketh, Head of market development UK at PEXA, highlights the transformative impact of digital advancements on the UK property market. PEXA, a digital platform, is revolutionizing property transactions by providing a secure and efficient digital workspace for conveyancers, lenders, and other stakeholders. This collaborative approach addresses the fragmented processes of the past, which often led to communication delays and misaligned workflows, introducing risks and inefficiencies. PEXA s platform ensures real-time collaboration among all parties involved in a property transaction, significantly improving alignment and reducing potential errors and fraud. The platform also automates the settlement process, securely managing the flow of funds to the intended destination upon settlement. Integration with the Land Registry further ensures title security at the point of settlement. Beyond its platform, PEXA is contributing to the broader effort to modernize the UK s property transaction landscape. As a founding executive board member of the Open Property Data Association (OPDA), PEXA is driving the digitization of the home buying and selling process. The Property Data Trust Framework, introduced by the OPDA, aims to standardize property data handling across the industry, protecting consumers and streamlining the transaction process. PEXA s commitment to a more secure and efficient market is further demonstrated through its adoption of ISO 20022, an international standard for financial messaging. This standard improves the accuracy and efficiency of financial transactions, particularly in property transactions, where large sums of money are exchanged. In summary, PEXA s contributions are paving the way for a safer, more reliable future in property transactions. By building a resilient conveyancing process, PEXA is helping to ensure the industry s progress towards managing property transactions with the highest security and collaboration, benefiting all involved. The UK property market is evolving towards greater security, transparency, and efficiency, with initiatives like PEXA s playing a crucial role in this transformation.
