SLB launches AI - powered Lumi platform

Global energy technology company SLB (NYSE: SLB) has launched the Lumi™ data and AI platform, integrating advanced AI capabilities, including generative AI, with workflows across the energy value chain. The platform, built on industry standards, is available on major cloud service providers and on-premises, catering to SLB s global customer base. The Lumi platform unlocks access to high-quality data across subsurface, surface, planning, and operations, enhancing cross-domain collaboration and generating new intelligence and insights for enterprise-level decision-making. It incorporates large language models (LLMs) and domain foundation models from SLB, enabling customers to scale AI adoption at scale. SLB s Delfi™ digital platform will also benefit from the Lumi platform s data and machine learning capabilities, enhancing reservoir modeling, seismic interpretation, and operational efficiencies. The platform liberates data from structured and unstructured sources using standard protocols and complies with cybersecurity standards.
