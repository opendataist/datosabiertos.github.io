Amazon opening new data centre in Auckland , creating 1000 jobs

A new cloud region in New Zealand will see it invest around $7.5 billion into the country over the next 15 years, Amazon has announced. The government has welcomed the announcement, boasting it's "another major digital investment" earned by the Covid-19 response to the nation.
