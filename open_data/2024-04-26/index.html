"Empowering Sustainability: One Click LCA Acquires Buildrz for AI-Driven Green Building Solutions"

Published: 2024-04-26

One Click LCA, a company that helps with sustainability, recently bought Buildrz, a firm from Paris that uses AI for real estate. This helps users do studies and carbon checks with AI, finding eco-friendly projects early on and following rules. By adding Buildrz’s tech, One Click LCA wants to give global solutions for efficient and green building, with a focus on being clear and open about new ideas for a greener world.

The Ceph Foundation has a new version of their storage system called Ceph Squid, with better performance and support for protocols. Squid has upgrades like BlueStore and CRUSH improvements, along with better read speed using Crimson. This is a big deal as Ceph now has 1 exabyte of storage in 3,000 groups, showing it can grow and stay reliable. Also, new Diamond Members like Bloomberg and IBM have joined the Ceph Foundation, showing more interest in working together in the Ceph group, encouraging sharing and teamwork among developers and fans.

Next year, Cephalocon 2024 will happen in Geneva, giving pros in the industry a chance to talk about open data and storage in the future. This event shows how important it is to have free access to info and how working together can lead to new ideas and growth.

These changes show how vital it is to have easy, clear, and shareable data to help with sustainability and new ideas in all fields. Companies that use open data are leading the way to a more eco-friendly and team-focused future, making sure the world is better for those to come.
