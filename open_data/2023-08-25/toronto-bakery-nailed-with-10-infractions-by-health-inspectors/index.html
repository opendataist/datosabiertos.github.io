Toronto bakery nailed with 10 infractions by health inspectors

A bakery in Toronto has been given a conditional pass notice after inspectors discovered 10 infractions in the food-handling room. Warnings are being issued to the city s health watchdog, which says it has revealed more than 100 cases.
