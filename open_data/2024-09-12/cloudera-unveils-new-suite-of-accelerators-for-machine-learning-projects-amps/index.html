Cloudera Unveils New Suite of Accelerators for Machine Learning Projects ( AMPs )

Cloudera, a hybrid platform for data, analytics, and AI, has announced new Accelerators for ML Projects (AMPs) to expedite enterprise AI use cases and reduce deployment time. These AMPs, showcased at EVOLVE24 in Dubai, offer cutting-edge AI techniques and examples to assist AI integration and drive impactful results. The latest AMPs include: 1. Fine-Tuning Studio: An application and ecosystem for managing, fine-tuning, and evaluating Large Language Models (LLMs). 2. RAG with Knowledge Graph: Demonstrates how to power a Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) application with a knowledge graph to capture relationships and context not easily accessible by vector stores alone. 3. PromptBrew: Provides AI-powered assistance to create high-performing and reliable prompts via a simple user interface. 4. Chat with Your Documents: Enhances the responses of the LLM using context from an internal knowledge base created from the documents uploaded by the user. Cloudera s AMPs are fully open source and include deployment instructions for any environment. The company aims to make AI more accessible for businesses to accelerate adoption and maximize the value of their data and AI outputs. Cloudera will be showcasing these updates at EVOLVE24, where attendees can learn more about strategies to enhance productivity in the era of Generative AI. About Cloudera: Cloudera is the only true hybrid platform for data, analytics, and AI. With 100x more data under management than other cloud-only vendors, Cloudera empowers global enterprises to transform data of all types into valuable, trusted insights. The world s largest brands in various industries rely on Cloudera to use their data to solve complex problems. For more information, visit Cloudera.com and follow them on LinkedIn and X.
