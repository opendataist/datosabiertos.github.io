Trusted data today fuels tomorrow AI at Cloudera EVOLVE24 Dubai event - News

Cloudera, a pioneering hybrid platform for data, analytics, and AI, is organizing the EVOLVE24 conference in Dubai on September 12, 2024. The event aims to bring together industry leaders, customers, and partners to explore strategies for enhancing data-driven insights and productivity in the age of generative AI. EVOLVE24 will feature breakout sessions, keynote speeches, and hands-on workshops, focusing on the importance of modern data architecture, the advantages of hybrid cloud, and how these elements can speed up enterprise AI. Sponsored by IBM, Amazon Web Services (AWS), and Red Hat, the conference will address challenges and barriers in enterprise AI adoption, hybrid data management benefits, and the current state of data infrastructures. Cloudera s CEO, Charles Sansbury, along with other senior executives, will share their expertise and insights from various industries, including financial services, telecommunications, and manufacturing. EVOLVE24 Dubai offers real-world insights and practical applications, providing hands-on training and direct access to Cloudera experts. The multi-day, multi-city global conference aims to connect Cloudera s customers worldwide with leading experts in data management, analytics, and AI. With over 25 exabytes of data under management, Cloudera stands as a robust hybrid open data lakehouse for analytics and AI. The platform enables companies to efficiently and securely organize and manage large volumes of data, ensuring its accuracy and reliability for analysis and AI-powered decision-making. For more information about EVOLVE Dubai, visit www.cloudera.com/events/evolve. (Note: The Google Adsense code snippets have been removed from the summary to maintain focus on the content.)
