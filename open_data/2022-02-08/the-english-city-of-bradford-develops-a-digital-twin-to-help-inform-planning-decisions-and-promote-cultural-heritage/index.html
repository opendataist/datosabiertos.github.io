The English city of Bradford develops a digital twin to help inform planning decisions and promote cultural heritage

Bradford is to be transformed into a virtual city centre, which aims to improve the city’s planning and air pollution levels, researchers have revealed. They say the project is “a revolution in terms of city planners” and could help the creation of green space.
