Blacks In Technology and The Linux Foundation Partner to Offer up to $100 , 000 in Training & Certification to Deserving Individuals

A new scholarship programme is being launched to help more Black individuals get started with a computer skills career, according to the largest community of Black technologists globally, the Blacks In Technology Foundation and the Linux Foundation. Each recipient will have six months to sit for their exams at no charge.
