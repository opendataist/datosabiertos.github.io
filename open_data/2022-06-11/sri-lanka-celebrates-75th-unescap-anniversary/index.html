Sri Lanka celebrates 75th UNESCAP Anniversary

The Sri Lankan government has welcomed the 75th anniversary of the United Nations Economic and Social Commission for Asia and the Pacific (UNESCAP) which was held on hybrid mode in Bangkok on May 23-27. Following the celebrations, the government visited the country for the first time.
