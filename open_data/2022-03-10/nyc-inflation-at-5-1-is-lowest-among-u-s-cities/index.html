NYC Inflation at 5 . 1 % Is Lowest Among U . S . Cities

New York City is one of the most expensive places to earn a living in the US, according to data published by the Bureau of Labor Statistics. The city has been rising at the slowest pace of any US metro area, compared with the decades-high rate of inflation.
