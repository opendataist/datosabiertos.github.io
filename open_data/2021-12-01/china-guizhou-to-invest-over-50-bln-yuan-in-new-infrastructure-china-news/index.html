China Guizhou to invest over 50 bln yuan in new infrastructure - China News

China's Guizhou Province has announced plans to invest more than 50 billion yuan (about 7.9 billion U.S. dollars) in new infrastructure to promote the development of an international open data platform for the Chinese Five-hundred-meter Aperture Spherical Radio Telescope (FAST).
