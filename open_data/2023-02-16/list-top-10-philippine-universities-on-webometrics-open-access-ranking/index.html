LIST : Top 10 Philippine Universities on Webometrics Open Access Ranking

The University of the Philippines Diliman has been named the top 10 universities in the world’s largest academic ranking of higher education institutions (HEIs) which measure open access to knowledge generated by the internet. They also have been ranked first in a list of 31,000 heis from 200 countries.
