Finally , you can buy Solidworks online - Engineering . com

Solidworks CEO Manish Kumar announced a new sales strategy for Solidworks 2025, allowing customers to purchase the software directly online. This marks a significant shift from the traditional model where purchases were made through Solidworks resellers. The latest version of Solidworks, set to release on November 17, 2024, will feature three main versions: 3DExperience Solidworks, Solidworks with Cloud Services, and Solidworks Cloud Offer, each with Standard, Professional, and Premium tiers. A new Ultimate tier will also be introduced. Kumar emphasized that despite the new online purchasing option, Solidworks resellers will still play a crucial role in providing consultative selling and onboarding support. The software will continue to rely on the 3DExperience platform for data storage and sharing. In terms of new features, Solidworks 2025 will offer improved collaboration and performance, including multi-approval timestamps, real-time notifications, and faster edge selection for chamfer commands. The Ultimate tier will also include cloud-based design, simulation, product lifecycle management, marketing, documentation, and manufacturing tools. Overall, the new sales strategy and features in Solidworks 2025 aim to provide more transparency, convenience, and value to customers, while still maintaining the support and expertise of Solidworks resellers.
