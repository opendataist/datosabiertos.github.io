The UAE's Financial Performance in Late 2023 and The Drum's Commitment to Data Privacy

Published: 2024-03-17

The UAE has improved how it handles money. It made AED155.9 billion and spent AED131.3 billion in the last part of 2023. They want to get money from different places and use their money well. They share a report with important info for studying finances and monitoring economic policies.

The Drum website, run by Carnyx Group Ltd, cares about keeping personal info safe. They have rules about how they collect, store, and use personal data. They focus on user rights and data security. They are clear about how they share data, how long they keep it, and if they send it to other countries. Users can control their info and complain if needed.

It’s important to share info openly in today’s digital world. When info is easy to find, share, and use, it helps build trust, make people accountable, and create positive changes. By working towards a future where info is open and easy to access, we can create a society that is more informed and empowered. This will leave a legacy of transparency and openness for future generations.
