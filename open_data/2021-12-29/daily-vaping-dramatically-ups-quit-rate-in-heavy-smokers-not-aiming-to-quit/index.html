Daily vaping dramatically ups quit rate in heavy smokers not aiming to quit

A new study has found that adult smokers with no plans to quit smoking e-cigarettes are more likely to switch to daily vaping than those who didn't use traditional tobacco when they were not using their own cigarettes earlier this year.
