China plans to reduce output of steel , aluminum to achieve carbon neutrality

The price of steel in China has fallen to its highest level for more than a decade, according to the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, as the country's steel industry tries to reduce carbon dioxide emissions by 2030 and carbon neutrality by 2060.
