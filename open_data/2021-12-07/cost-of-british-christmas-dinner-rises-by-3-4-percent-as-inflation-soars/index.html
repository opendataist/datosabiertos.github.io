Cost of British Christmas Dinner Rises by 3 . 4 Percent as Inflation Soars

The cost of a Christmas turkey dinner has risen by more than 3% in the four weeks to November 28 compared with the same period last year, according to the UK's biggest supermarket chain, Kantar Statistics. They say they are still struggling to keep their spending on the festive season.
