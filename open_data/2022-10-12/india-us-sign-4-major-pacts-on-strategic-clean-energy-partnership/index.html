India , US Sign 4 Major Pacts On Strategic Clean Energy Partnership

India and the US have inked four major MoUs in the field of oil & gas to intensify the bilateral strategic clean energy partnership, indicating how both countries can leverage the strength of the private sector to solve issues pertaining to climate action and energy security.In Houston, India's minister of Oil and Natural Gas has launched a special Methane Round 2022.
