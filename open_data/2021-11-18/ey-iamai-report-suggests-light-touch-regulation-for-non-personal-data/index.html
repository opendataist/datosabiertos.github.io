EY - IAMAI report suggests light touch regulation for Non Personal Data

Data firms in India have expressed mixed opinions about the need for a separate non-personal data regulation framework, according to reports from the European Union of Data (EY) and the Medianama Association of India (Amai). They say they have no agreement.
