Russia Volma Group acquires gypsum plant with quarry in Kazakhstan

A Russian company has acquired 100% of a gypsum plant in Kazakhstan, according to reports released by the Russian state media on Monday, 26 September 2023. The company says it is buying the firm s entire production divisions in the region of Atyrau.
