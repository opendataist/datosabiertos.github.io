Investigator - Analyst . Prosecutorial Vetting Secretariat

Global Solutions Ventures (GSV), a joint venture between ZemiTek, LLC and Dexis Consulting Group, is seeking qualified applicants for the Prosecutor Vetting Commission Secretariat. The Investigator/Analyst role involves conducting research and background checks for vetting candidates, working with technical teams and Commission panels as per Law 252/2023. Applicants must possess a relevant degree, experience in accessing and analyzing national databases, and proficiency in English and Microsoft office programs. To apply, candidates must submit a current CV, a cover letter in English, a list of three professional references, a declaration about their family s professional background, and a copy of their national ID and passport photo page. The application process includes a background check by Moldovan authorities, and candidates must not engage in other employment during the contract period. Applicants must also provide consent for a background check, acknowledging that their personal data, including that of their immediate family members, will be accessed and analyzed by competent authorities. The results will be shared with the Commission and Anne M. Trice, Senior Director at Dexis Consulting Group. The position requires eagerness to learn, flexibility, teamwork, and excellent communication skills. Disqualifying factors include current or past work in the Moldovan prosecution system or being a student at the NIJ. Job offers are contingent upon a complete application packet, a background check, and three positive professional reference checks.
