Oyo Pledges to Deploy ICT for Infrastructure Development

The Oyo State government has said it has digitised many government services in the past two years, including land procurement, survey and certificate of ownership processes, in a bid to boost the digital economy in its northern state. They have also rolled out data warehousing and infrastructure.
