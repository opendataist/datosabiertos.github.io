UN Globalist Pact for the Future Is a Dangerous Power Grab

Nations are urged to divest from and dismantle the United Nations (UN), which is seen as the driving force behind globalism and global governance. Critics argue that the UN s Pact for the Future undermines U.S. sovereignty by allowing international organizations to declare a global state of emergency without consent. The pact is viewed as a step towards a technocratic world government, ruled by unaccountable, unelected bureaucrats. The UN s 16 sustainable goals for the future are criticized for promoting Marxist tenets, such as DEI (diversity, equity, and inclusion), gender transitioning, and potential health mandates. The goals are said to be implemented behind closed doors, without a democratic vote. The Pact for the Future is also seen as a threat to sovereignty and borders, with the UN seeking financial support from member countries. Critics argue that the UN has historically caused more problems and condemned free nations, likening it to a Dictators Club. Despite the criticism, the Pact for the Future includes commitments to improve the effectiveness and representativeness of the Security Council, nuclear disarmament, and the governance of outer space. It also aims to reform the international financial architecture to better serve developing countries, address climate change, and promote digital cooperation and human rights. The Pact also includes a Declaration on Future Generations, which calls for accounting for future generations in decision-making and more meaningful opportunities for young people to participate in global governance. In summary, while the Pact for the Future contains ambitious goals and commitments, critics argue that it undermines national sovereignty, promotes undemocratic decision-making, and threatens free nations. The UN is urged to divest from and dismantle the organization to prevent further erosion of national autonomy.
