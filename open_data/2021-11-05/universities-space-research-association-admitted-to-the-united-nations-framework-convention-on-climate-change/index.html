Universities Space Research Association Admitted to the United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change

The United Nations has announced the admission of a university space research association as an official observer for the UN climate change summit in Glasgow, Scotland, in November 2021. The USRA delegation was warmly applauded by the delegates at COP26 when it joined the COP 26 council chamber.
