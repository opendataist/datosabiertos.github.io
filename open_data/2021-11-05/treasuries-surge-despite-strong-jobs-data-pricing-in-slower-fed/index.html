Treasuries Surge Despite Strong Jobs Data , Pricing In Slower Fed

The US Treasury yields fell as much as 7.8 basis points to 1.45%, falling below its 50-and 200-day moving averages for the first time since September, according to the Bloomberg Financial Monitoring Service (Bloomberg) . The UK bond market is on track for its biggest decline in more than a year.
