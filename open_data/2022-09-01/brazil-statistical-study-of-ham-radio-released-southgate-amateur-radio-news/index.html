Brazil : Statistical study of ham radio released | Southgate Amateur Radio News

The Brazilian Association of Radio Broadcasting and Radio Research (LAbre) has published a new study on amateur radio in the state of São Paulo. The most obvious is that the number of radio amateurs in Brazil has increased since last year, making an order growth of 2.2%.
