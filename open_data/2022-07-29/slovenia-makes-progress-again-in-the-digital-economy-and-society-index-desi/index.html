Slovenia makes progress again in the Digital Economy and Society Index ( DESI )

Slovenia remains above the European average in the digital economy and society, according to new data published by the Department for Digital Transformation ( DESI). The latest index reveals that the country has moved up two places ahead of France, Lithuania and Hungary.
