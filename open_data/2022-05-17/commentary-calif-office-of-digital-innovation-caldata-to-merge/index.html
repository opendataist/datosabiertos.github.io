Commentary : Calif . Office of Digital Innovation , CalData to Merge

California’s governor has proposed to merge the Office of Digital Innovation (ODI) with CalData. The merger will work to improve Californians’ experience of their government through championing human-centric design and innovative use of technology, according to the chief data officer.
