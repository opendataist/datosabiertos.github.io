SANSA joins other Partners in the 10th Space Apps Challenge

This year’s NASA International Space Apps Challenge will commemorate ten incredible years of community, collaboration and problem-solving across the globe, with the launch of a new initiative to help solve space challenges in the space industry. Each year, the event will be held at the US National Space Agency (NASA).
