Dubai secondary real estate market booms in first quarter

Dubai’s real estate sector recorded the best ever quarter since 2010, with a total of 21,039 sales transactions valued at Dh55.5 billion, according to the latest figures from the consultancy Zoom Property Insights.In the first three months of the year, there were more foreign investors than ever before.
