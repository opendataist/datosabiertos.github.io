OkCredit starts CoAid website to help search for Covid - 19 medicines , equipment

India's largest pharmacy company has launched a new search tool to help people find Covid-19 medicines and treatment equipment across the country, including oxygen and beds and oximeters, in the wake of the coronavirus pandemic, it has been announced by the company.
