Artsakh Parliament Speaker calls on international organizations to prevent destruction of Armenian monuments

Armenia's parliament has appealed to the UN, UNESCO and the World Council of Churches over the danger threatening the Armenian cultural and religious heritage in the area of the Nagorno-Karabakh conflict which has come under Azerbaijani military occupation.
