Fostering Collaboration and Transparency in Data-Driven Decision Making

Published: 2024-03-22

In today’s world, open data and transparency are very important. People are talking about regulating AI technology and promoting sustainability. This means sharing information that everyone can access and work together on.

Esri Canada bought Ratio.City to help with Canada’s housing crisis. They are using open data to plan things transparently. This helps everyone work together for the greater good.

Governor Tina Kotek wants to make a plan for AI in Oregon. She wants to make sure AI is used fairly and equally. The council she leads focuses on diversity and working together to understand AI better.

Researchers in Spain are studying how things like money and vaccines affect the spread of COVID-19. They are using open data to find answers. They are also using a tool called REDCapDM to manage data better for research.

In Nigeria, it’s important to collect data honestly and report it accurately. This builds trust and accountability. Using technology and working together can help with challenges like bad data quality.

As we make decisions based on data and technology, it’s important to be open and work together. This helps create a society where everyone can access and use data for progress and new ideas.
